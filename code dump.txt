Here is a draft you can use. It is structured to show that you have reviewed their data but need these specific "depth" markers to provide a precise and competitive proposal.
Subject: Clarifying Questions: RFP for Fund Accounting Services – [Client Name / Fund Name] CCF
Dear [Contact Name],
Thank you for providing the initial details regarding the fund holdings, share classes, and investor counts for the upcoming CCF mandate. This data provides a helpful baseline for our initial assessment.
To ensure we provide the most accurate and comprehensive proposal—particularly given the unique tax-transparent nature of a Common Contractual Fund (CCF)—we would appreciate your insights on the following clarifying points:
1. Tax Transparency & Reporting
 * Tax Jurisdictions: In which specific jurisdictions are the underlying investors tax-resident? This will help us confirm the tax treaty applications required for the "look-through" process.
 * Reporting Frequency: What is the required frequency for investor-level tax reporting (e.g., daily, monthly, or annual)?
 * Withholding Tax (WHT): Do you expect the Fund Accountant to manage the WHT reclaim process directly, or will you be utilizing an external tax advisor for these filings?
 * Income Equalization: Does the fund require income equalization accounting for subscriptions and redemptions?
2. Portfolio & Transactional Complexity
 * Trade Volume: What is the estimated average monthly trade volume (number of tickets) for the portfolio?
 * Asset Types: Beyond standard equities/bonds, does the portfolio include OTC derivatives, private credit, or complex bank debts that require specialized valuation?
 * Corporate Actions: Are there any specific emerging market exposures that may require high-touch monitoring for complex corporate actions?
3. Investor & NAV Operations
 * NAV Timeline: What is the required delivery window for the Net Asset Value (e.g., T+0, T+1, etc.)?
 * Bespoke Reporting: Do any specific investors require "look-through" transparency for regulatory reporting, such as Solvency II or specific ESG disclosures?
 * Connectivity: Are there specific custodians or third-party platforms that we would be required to build automated data feeds for?
We would be happy to jump on a brief 15-minute call to discuss these points if that is more convenient for your team. Alternatively, feel free to provide the responses via email at your earliest convenience.
Best regards,
[Your Name] [Your Title]
[Your Company]
